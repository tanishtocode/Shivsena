Our Story

Why शिव सेतू Was Created

शिव सेतू was founded by a dedicated social worker who saw people in the community struggling to get their basic problems heard and resolved.

From broken roads to water supply issues, people often had nowhere to go or no easy way to follow up.

This platform was created to give every citizen a simple, accessible way to raise their voice — and to make sure that voice is heard by the right people.

We believe a strong community is built when its people have the tools to speak up and when those in power are held accountable.
